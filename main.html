Overview

Forell-Agency is your trusted partner in seamless guest management for events of any scale. We help event hosts simplify the invitation process, track attendance, and deliver a premium welcome experience for every guest. From account creation to guest verification at the event venue, we provide a modern, secure, and efficient solution that elevates your entire event experience. we offer services such as;

Guest Invitation Management
We handle the full process of sending personalized invitations to your guests, complete with unique access codes for quick entry on the event day.
Secure Guest Verification
Each guest receives a randomly generated code, ensuring only verified invitees gain access to your event. Our system supports fast and seamless check-in at the venue.
Event Data Collection
Hosts can create an account and provide key event details. We ensure accurate guest data management to support smooth planning and coordination.
Automated Approval & Notification
Once a host submits their event details, our team reviews and approves the request. A customized invitation link is then generated and sent directly to the host for distribution.
Real-Time Guest Tracking
Monitor confirmed, pending, and attended guests in real time for better event flow and organization.

How It Works

1

Create an Account

Event hosts sign up on the platform and provide basic information about their event.

2

Submit Event Details

Hosts fill in the required data, including event type, date, venue, and guest list.

3

Agency Review & Approval

Forell-Agency reviews the submitted information and approves the request.

4

Payment for service

After approval,the host then pays to the company account for service to be rendered.

5

Invitation Link is Generated

A custom invitation page and unique codes are automatically created for your guests.

6

Share with Your Guests

Hosts receive a secure link that they can send to all guests as their official invitation.

7

Guest Arrival & Verification

On the event day, guests present their unique codes at the entrance for quick and organized check-in by the ushers.
